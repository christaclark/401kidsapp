Why use 401(K)ids?

We are a mobile app that offers a fun way for kids to manage their allowance while learning how to save, budget, and responsibly spend their money. We want to empower kids to feel like they have ownership over their money, by choosing how they earn and spend it.

Parents, you get to create chores or ways for your child to make money and say how much each task is worth, and then offer different ways for your child to spend the money made (for example, extra time on video games or saving up for an iPad).
